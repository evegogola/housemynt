Common Questions About Fees

Are there any setup or onboarding fees?

Yes, we charge a $99 setup fee. You only pay the monthly management fee once your property is rented.

What is the fee based on?

The percentage is based on the monthly rent collected. So if your property rents for $3,000/month on the Standard plan, our fee is $400/month.

Are maintenance costs included?

Maintenance and repair costs are passed through to the owner at actual cost. We coordinate everything — you just approve the work.
