Best Practices

The standards and practices we follow to protect your property, your tenants, and your investment.

Our Standards

How We Protect Your Investment

At House Mynt, best practices aren't just a checklist — they're how we operate every single day.

Tenant Screening

🔍

Thorough Background Checks

We run full credit, criminal, and eviction history checks on every applicant.

💼

Income Verification

We verify employment and income to ensure tenants can comfortably afford the rent.

📞

Landlord Reference Checks

We contact previous landlords to confirm rental history and payment behavior.

Property Condition Walkthroughs

📸

Move-In Documentation

We conduct a detailed move-in walkthrough with photos to document the property's condition.

🏠

Periodic Walkthroughs

We schedule regular property condition walkthroughs to catch maintenance issues early.

✅

Move-Out Inspection

A thorough move-out walkthrough ensures accurate security deposit accounting.

Our Commitment to California Law

California landlord-tenant law is constantly evolving. House Mynt stays current on all state and local regulations — including AB 1482 rent control, security deposit rules, habitability requirements, and the latest 2026 tenant protection laws.

Maintenance & Vendors

🔧

Licensed & Insured Vendors Only

We work exclusively with licensed, insured contractors and vendors.

⚡

Prompt Response to Maintenance

Emergency issues are addressed immediately; routine repairs are handled efficiently.

💰

Cost Transparency

We get owner approval for non-emergency repairs above your pre-set threshold.

Financial Management

🏦

Trust Account Management

All tenant funds are held in a separate California DRE-compliant trust account.

📊

Monthly Statements

You receive clear, itemized monthly statements showing all income, expenses, and your net disbursement.

📅

On-Time Owner Disbursements

We process owner payments on a consistent schedule every month.
