How We Work

A simple, stress-free process from start to finish. Here's what to expect when you work with House Mynt.

Our Process

1

Free Consultation

We start with a conversation to learn about your property, your goals, and your timeline. No pressure, no obligation — just honest advice from a local expert.

2

Property Walkthrough

We visit your property to assess its condition, discuss any needed repairs, and determine the right rental price for the current South Bay market.

3

Listing & Marketing

We professionally photograph your property and list it across all major rental platforms to attract qualified tenants quickly.

4

Tenant Screening

We thoroughly screen all applicants — credit, income, rental history, and background checks — to find you the best possible tenant.

5

Lease & Move-In

We prepare the lease, handle the move-in walkthrough, collect the security deposit, and make sure everything is documented properly.

6

Ongoing Management

From rent collection and maintenance coordination to tenant communication and monthly statements — we handle everything so you don't have to.

Our Promise to You

We treat your property like our own. Transparent communication, honest pricing, and reliable service — every single month.
